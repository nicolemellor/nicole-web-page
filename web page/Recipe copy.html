Wanna Bake Some Cookies?

Well... If You Do Heres A Recipe!

These warm, soft but crispy cookies are good for a cold day to warm you up. These cookies are very enjoyable while dipping with milk. Eating many at a time may give you a stomach ache, but its defiantly worth it.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3/4 cup packed dark brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 12 oz. bag semisweet chocolate chips
  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon fine salt

Directions

  1. Evenly position 2 racks in the middle of the oven and preheat to 375 degrees F
  2. line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper or silicone sheets
  3. Put the butter in a microwave safe bowl, cover and microwave on medium power until melted
  4. Whisk the sugars, eggs, butter and vanilla in a large bowl until smooth
  5. Whisk the flour, baking soda and salt in another bowl
  6. Stir the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients with a wooden spoon
  7. Stir in the chocolate chips
  8. Scoop heaping tablespoons of the dough onto the prepared pans
  9. Wet hands slightly and roll the dough into balls
  10. Space the cookies about 2-inches apart on the pans
  11. Bake, until golden, but still soft in the center, 12 to 16 minutes
  12. Transfer hot cookies with a spatula to a rack to cool
  13. Serve

Prep Time: 20 Cook Time: 15

Serve with warm or cold milk. Bon appetit!!!
